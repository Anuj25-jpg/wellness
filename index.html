Beginner Yoga Poses

Downward-Facing Dog

A full-body stretch that calms the mind and energizes the body.

Instructions:

  • Start on your hands and knees.
  • Tuck your toes, lift your hips up and back, forming an inverted 'V'.
  • Keep your head between your upper arms.

Tree Pose

Improves balance, focus, and strengthens your legs and core.

Instructions:

  • Stand tall and shift your weight to your right foot.
  • Place your left foot on your inner right thigh or calf (avoid the knee).
  • Bring your hands together in front of your chest.

Warrior II

Builds strength and confidence, while stretching your hips and shoulders.

Instructions:

  • Step your feet wide apart.
  • Turn your right foot out 90 degrees and bend your right knee.
  • Extend your arms parallel to the floor and gaze forward.

Cat-Cow Stretch

Warms the spine and relieves tension in the back and neck.

Instructions:

  • Start on your hands and knees in a tabletop position.
  • Inhale as you drop your belly and look up (Cow).
  • Exhale as you round your spine and tuck your chin (Cat).

Cobra Pose

Strengthens the spine and opens the chest and shoulders.

Instructions:

  • Lie on your stomach with your hands under your shoulders.
  • Press through your hands to lift your chest off the floor.
  • Keep your hips on the floor and your shoulders relaxed.

Child's Pose

A gentle resting pose that helps to relieve stress and tension.

Instructions:

  • Kneel on the floor and sit back on your heels.
  • Fold forward, resting your forehead on the floor.
  • Let your arms rest alongside your body, palms up.

Guided Meditation

Morning Mindfulness (5 Min)

Find a quiet space. On your phone, play your favorite calming song. Close your eyes and focus on your breath to set a positive intention for the day.

Stress Relief Breath (10 Min)

Feeling overwhelmed? Choose a soothing track on your phone. Follow a simple box breathing pattern: inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, and hold for 4.

Sleep Meditation (15 Min)

To prepare for deep sleep, listen to calming ambient sounds on your phone. Quiet your mind, relax your body, and allow yourself to drift off peacefully.

Focus Boost (5 Min)

Before an important task, put on an instrumental track. Close your eyes and visualize success. This short break can significantly sharpen your concentration.
